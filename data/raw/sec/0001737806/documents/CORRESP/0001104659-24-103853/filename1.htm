Revenue Overview

 

The Company serves third-party merchants in China who sell their products on the Company’s online platforms. Its revenues primarily consist of (i) transaction services and (ii) online marketing services and others.

 

For transaction services, the Company earns fees from merchants for sales of their products completed on its platforms. At no point during these transactions does the Company take control of the products sold by merchants.

 

For online marketing services and others, the Company earns service fees from merchants for various types of online marketing services. These include matching product listings appearing in search or browsing results on the Company’s platforms, charging merchants based on impressions or clicks, and providing display marketing services where merchants place advertisements on the Company’s platforms primarily at fixed prices.

 

Due to the differences in the nature of these services, the Company disaggregates its revenue by type of service into two categories: online marketing services and others, and transaction services.

Geographical region, market or type of customer

Since the merchant support team supports merchants on both platforms in a similar nature, and many merchants operate on both platforms, we operate under one sales channel for services to merchants.
