On February 8, 2019, the registered follow-on public offering of an aggregate of 55,000,000 American depositary shares (“ADSs”), each representing four Class A ordinary shares of the Company, by the Company and certain selling shareholders was priced at US$25.00 per ADS.

 

The Company will issue and sell 40,185,000 ADSs and the selling shareholders will sell an aggregate of 14,815,000 ADSs. The Company has granted to the underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to 8,250,000 additional ADSs, and such optional ADSs, upon exercise, will solely be sold by the Company.

 

The gross proceeds to the Company will be approximately US$1.2 billion, assuming the underwriters fully exercise their option to purchase additional shares. The Company will not receive any proceeds from the sale of the ADSs by the selling shareholders.
